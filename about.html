OUTCOMES is a strategic storytelling collective.

SHANNON KNAPP believes in disrupting the marketing status quo, and striving to create spaces for the equitable flow of ideas. Shannon has spent the last decade developing plays and irreverent publicity schemes with her close collaborators at Non-State Actors, the Pittsburgh Classic Players, and folkLAB, as well as working frequently with off the WALL productions and Throughline Theatre Company.
CONNOR SHOISHITA PICKETT is a lifetime creative who specializes in consensus-building, feedback facilitation, and small teams leadership. He has created works for stage and screen, led community and ensemble initiatives, and taught writing & performance.
